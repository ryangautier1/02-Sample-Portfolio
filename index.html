About Me

My name is Ryan Gautier and I am a student at the University of Texas Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. I am from Frisco, TX, and have lived in Austin since the summer of 2017. I studied mechanical engineering at Texas A&M University, where I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in May of 2017. Following graduation, I worked as an engineering technician, where I assembled power supply equipment for semiconductor manufacturing machinery. After doing that work for about a year, I took a position as an electrical utilities design engineer. My role in that position was to manage up to seventy projects at a time, doing the engineering and administrative work involved for each project. I held that position for about a year and a half, until I finally decided to change my career path toward web development.

My first exposure to coding was in my undergraduate education, where I learned Matlab. I used Matlab to develop various programs, such as simulating a car's suspension system response over vairous road conditions. I continued studying various coding languages in my free time, and finally decided to persue a web development career. I enjoy coding because of the feeling of satisfaction I get when my code works the way it is intended to. I am set up to graduate from my coding bootcamp on June 1st, 2020. I am excited to see what my future has in store for me in the field of web development!
